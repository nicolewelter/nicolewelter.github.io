The Shave Project

Packaging

Problem

Lack of Gender Neutral / Eco-friendly shaving options.

Client

Members of the LGBTQ+ community who want a neutral shaving option.

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop, and Lightroom

Solution

Create a non-gender based shave company that gives the users an eco friendly option to maintain their own shaving tools.

The Common Chow

Branding

Problem

Lack of Student Awareness

Client

EWU Food Pantry

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop, and Lightroom

Solution

Create a fun and engaging branding through the use of a modern hipster aesthetic to create a brand more suitable for college students. The goal in creating this brand was to give students using the food pantries something clean and modern to belong to and make them feel apart of.
